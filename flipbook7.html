Modern Day Frieze

by Fairus Nahiyan

Oil painting is more than just an art form for me—it's a therapeutic process that allows me to express emotions and relive cherished memories. I often paint scenes from my hometown, as it holds a special place in my heart. The landscapes, streets, and familiar places bring back the best moments of my life, making each brushstroke a journey through time. The richness of oil paints helps me capture the warmth and nostalgia I feel, turning my canvas into a reflection of my deepest connections.
