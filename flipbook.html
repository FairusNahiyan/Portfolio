Modern Day Frieze

A Collection of Contemporary Textile Designs

This portfolio showcases a series of innovative textile patterns inspired by modern architectural elements and geometric forms. Each design represents a fusion of traditional craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetics.

Design Process

The designs in this collection were developed through a combination of digital and traditional techniques. Starting with hand-drawn sketches, each pattern was refined and digitally enhanced to create seamless, repeatable patterns suitable for textile production.

Materials and techniques were carefully selected to maintain the integrity of the original designs while ensuring practical application in modern textile manufacturing.

Pattern Development

Each pattern in this collection explores the relationship between space, form, and texture. The designs draw inspiration from architectural friezes, modernist patterns, and geometric abstractions found in contemporary urban environments.
