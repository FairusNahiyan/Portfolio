About Me

Hello! I'm Fairus Nahiyan, a textile designer passionate about creating patterns that bridge traditional craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetics.

My work focuses on exploring the intersection of cultural heritage and modern design principles, creating pieces that are both timeless and innovative.

With a background in textile design and years of experience in pattern creation, I strive to push the boundaries of conventional design while maintaining a deep respect for traditional techniques and motifs.

This portfolio represents a collection of my work, showcasing various projects and explorations in textile design. Each piece tells a story of careful consideration, artistic expression, and technical precision.
